Hanuman

He fighted with the 4 lions nicknamed "The Four Musketeers", who arrived to dethrone him.

Bibi

She was the matriarch of the troop and she had a cub that she lost. She challenged the 4 lions who arrived to challenge the dominant lions and took them away from the pride.

Sienna

She had two cubs but 1 died in a buffalo attack. She was one of Notch's daughters.

Claude

He joined "The Four Musketeers" and he died of a spear wound in a Maasai village.

"The Four Muskeeters"

They were named Scarface (scar on the right eye caused by a Maasai spear), Morani (more imposing), Hunter (lighter mane) and Sikio (cut-off right ear).

Bibi

She was the mother of 3 cubs, one entrusted to Sienna.

Alana

He was a 2-year-old male who would become a big lion and he was greedy. He died on December 7, 2015 after eating many vultures poisoned by farmers with Fuduran. He was attacked by buffaloes all night from December 6 to 7, 2015. The vets euthanized him to put him out of his misery.

Sienna

She was courageous and she led the family on hunting trips. She had 1 cub and her mother was Red. She disappeared and was found dead on December 6, 2015 by poison (Fuduran). She was devoured by hundreds of vultures, hyenas and buffalo, who also perished.

Bibi

She had disappeared and she died on December 6, 2015 of poison at the age of 17. She was the matriarch of the marsh troop and she had been seen panting with drool all over her mouth and lying on her side.
